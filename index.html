What is Save My Fridge ?

Donate and recover free food around you !

You are going on vacation and your fridge is full ? You have grown too many vegetables in your garden ? And you don't like to throw away food. On Save My Fridge, it's easy, by posting an ad, you can give away fresh vegetables, baby milk and any food that is not expired. All together, let's donate food to fight against food waste.
